Amenities

Pools

There are three pools to choose from if you don't count all the ponds! Each pool has massive pavillion which you can request to throw parties and also includes bathrooms. The pools are open from April 15th - October 15th.

Park

The main attraction is the park, centrally located in the middle of the neighboorhood. We have Food Truck Fridays throughout most of the year and our Social Committee throws awesome events for the kids. You'll also see organized fitness activites such as Yoga and soccer.

Playgrounds

There are four playgrounds; one at each pool and the another within the Park.

Dog Park

We even have our very own 1/4 acre fenced dog park! The shaded area is great for a hot summer day.

Trails

The entire neighborhood has sidewalks which in some areas are super-wide but perhaps you like to explore the scenery. There are several miles of short disconnected trails that weave throughout the neighborhood. Most go around the ponds so be careful of stumbling upon a gators!

Kayak Launch

There's a spot in the neighborhood to launch a kayak onto a 1.5mi section of Long Branch River which averages 35 feet wide and only 5 feet deep at mid-tide.

Fishing

There are over two dozen ponds, small and large, in the community and you frequently see people fishing. The ponds are stocked with triploid grass carp.

Greenway

Just across the front entrance of community, Savannah Highway, is access to the West Ashley Greenway, a 8 mile walking/biking trail. Take it all the way to Avondale!
