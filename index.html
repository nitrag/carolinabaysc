Amenities

Pools

There are three pools to choose from, available to all Carolina Bay homeowners regardless of which section you live in. Each pool has a pavillion which you can reserve to hold small events such as birthday parties and also includes restrooms. The pools open on April 15th and close October 15th.

Park

The main attraction is the park, conveniently located in the middle of the neighboorhood. We have Food Truck Fridays throughout most of the year and our Social Committee throws awesome events for the kids including Movies and carnival type activies. You'll also see adult organized fitness activites such as Yoga and soccer.

Playgrounds

There are four playgrounds; one at each pool and the another within the Park.

Dog Park

We even have our very own 1/4 acre fenced dog park! The shaded area is great for a hot summer day.

Trails

The entire neighborhood has sidewalks which in some areas are super-wide but perhaps you like to explore the scenery. There are several miles of short disconnected trails that weave throughout the neighborhood. Most go around the ponds so be careful of stumbling upon a gators!

Kayak Launch

There's a spot in the neighborhood to launch a kayak onto a 1.5mi section of Long Branch River which averages 35 feet wide and only 5 feet deep at mid-tide.

Fishing

There are about two dozen ponds, small and large, in the community and you frequently see people fishing. At least the main ones are stocked seasonally with fish such as triploid grass carp.

Active Community

There are over 1700 living units in Carolina Bay and still growing. With that there are over 1400 members in communication on private Facebook group which is a great way to stay involved in neighborhood news. NextDoor is active as well for the surrounding West Ashley neighborhoods.

Greenway

Just across the main entrance of community, at Savannah Highway, is access to the West Ashley Greenway, a 8 mile walking/biking trail. Take it all the way to Avondale!
